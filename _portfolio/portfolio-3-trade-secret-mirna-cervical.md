As part of a national research program in Colombia that I coordinated, funded by a grant from the Ministry of Science, Technology and Innovation (MinCiencias), our team developed a method for the identification of microRNAs to detect cervical cancer. The method was formally endorsed as a trade secret for intellectual property protection through the University of Antioquia's Knowledge Transfer Management Office (Innovation Division), with recognition from MinCiencias. For inquiries about this intellectual property, please reach out via the contact information on this site so I can help connect you with the appropriate technology transfer office.
