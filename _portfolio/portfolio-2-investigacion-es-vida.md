"Investigación es Vida" (Research is Life) was an outreach program, supported by the Mayor's Office of Medellín, that I participated in to support access to public education and bring scientific culture to underserved communities. Through the program, I helped motivate, inspire, and teach students, sharing hands-on research experiences to strengthen their interest in science and expand their vision of future academic and professional opportunities.
