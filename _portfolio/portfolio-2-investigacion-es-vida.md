"Investigación es Vida" (Research is Life) is an outreach initiative that I founded and currently direct with support from public and private institutions. Through this program, I create transformative scientific experiences for students from underserved communities, bringing world-class research directly to their neighborhoods and schools.

**Program Components:**

- **University Laboratory Tours**: I guide students through active research laboratories, offering hands-on exposure to cutting-edge scientific work and career pathways in research.
- **Inspirational and Motivational Workshops**: We deliver engaging workshops that combine theoretical foundations with hands-on practical applications, designed to ignite passion for science and demonstrate how research impacts real-world problems.
- **Interactive Experiments & Practical Training**: Students participate in authentic laboratory experiments, gaining practical research skills, scientific literacy, and confidence in STEM subjects.
- **Research Group Integration**: We facilitate opportunities for motivated students to join active research teams, providing mentorship and real-world research experience.
- **Mentorship & Career Development**: We work with students to explore career pathways in science and research, providing guidance on professional development, identifying opportunities in academia and industry, and connecting them with role models who have successfully pursued careers in STEM. Our mentors—professionals and graduate students who overcame socioeconomic barriers—share their journeys, challenges, and strategies for success, demonstrating that a fulfilling career in science is achievable regardless of background.

**Impact**: My mission is to ignite scientific curiosity, broaden students' vision of what's possible, and create pathways for talented young people from humble backgrounds to pursue careers in research and science. Since its inception, this initiative has reached hundreds of students, many of whom have gone on to pursue degrees in STEM fields at top universities.

**Current Collaboration**: We are currently working in collaboration with SEPREUN SAS in Medellín, expanding our reach through both in-person and online activities to serve even more students across different regions.
