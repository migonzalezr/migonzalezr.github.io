As a Titular Member of the Red Colombiana de Mujeres Científicas (Colombian Network of Women Scientists) since 2024, I serve as a workshop facilitator and guest speaker in public schools across Colombia through two of the network's outreach programs: "Científicas en tu Colegio" (Women Scientists in Your School) and "Científicas en el Exterior" (Women Scientists Abroad). Through these programs, I share my research journey and scientific career path with secondary school students, aiming to bring science closer to public education communities, inspire interest in STEM, and encourage young people — especially girls — to consider careers in science.
